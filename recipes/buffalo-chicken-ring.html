Buffalo Chicken Ring

Ingredients

  • 2 tubes crescent rolls
  • 4 cans cooked chicken
  • 2 blocks cream cheese
  • 1 bag sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 bag mozzarella cheese
  • 1 small bottle hot sauce
  • 1 bottle blue cheese
  • 1 bottle ranch

Instructions

  1. Grease large baking sheet with cooking spray, place an updside bowl in the middle. Arrange slices of crescent roll around the bowl
  2. Combine chicken, shredded cheese, cream cheese, and hot sauce in a large mixing bowl
  3. Use tablespoons to transfer contents onto the crescent rolls around the base of the bowl
  4. Carefully remove bowl
  5. Fold tips of crescent roll up into the middle and press down to secure
  6. Do this all the way around to solidify ring shape
  7. Cook for 25 minutes on 350
  8. Serve with blue cheese or ranch
