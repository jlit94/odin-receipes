Tortellini

Ingredients

  • 1 package tortellini
  • 1 container cherry tomatoes
  • 1 bag baby spinach
  • 1 pound ground hot italian sausage
  • 2 tablespoons garlic
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions

  1. Fill a medium sized sauce pan with a quart of water and bring to boil
  2. Add olive oil to medium skillet over medium to high height
  3. Once oil is hot, add in garlic and brown
  4. Add ground sausage to skillet and brown
  5. Water should be boiling, add tortellini to water while sausage browns
  6. Add spinach to skillet, cook down in batches if need be
  7. Add cherry tomatoes to skillet, lower heat to low and cook until tomatoes are sweating
  8. When tortellini are cooked, strain and add back to pot
  9. combine mixture from skillet to the pot with the tortellini and fold mixture in
  10. Serve with shredded parmesan on top
